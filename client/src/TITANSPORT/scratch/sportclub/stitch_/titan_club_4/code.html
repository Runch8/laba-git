Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes, our memberships are billed monthly. You can cancel or pause your membership at any time with a 30-day notice before your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 1-day guest pass for local residents to experience the Titan facility. Please contact the front desk to arrange your visit.

Do unused Personal Training sessions roll over?

Personal Training sessions included in the PRO and ELITE plans must be used within the billing month and do not roll over to the next month.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your membership tier at the start of any new billing cycle. The difference will be prorated accordingly.
